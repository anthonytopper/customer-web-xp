Psalms 93

93

1Jehovah reigneth; he is clothed with majesty;

Jehovah is clothed with strength; he hath girded himself therewith:

The world also is established, that it cannot be moved.

2Thy throne is established of old:

Thou art from everlasting.

3The floods have lifted up, O Jehovah,

The floods have lifted up their voice;

The floods lift up their awaves.

4Above the voices of many waters,

The mighty breakers of the sea,

Jehovah on high is mighty.

5Thy testimonies are very sure:

Holiness becometh thy house,

O Jehovah, for evermore.
