Psalms 100

100

A Psalm aof thanksgiving.

1Make a joyful noise unto Jehovah, aall ye lands.

2Serve Jehovah with gladness:

Come before his presence with singing.

3Know ye that Jehovah, he is God:

It is he that hath made us, aand we are his;

We are his people, and the sheep of his pasture.

4Enter into his gates with athanksgiving,

And into his courts with praise:

Give thanks unto him, and bless his name.

5For Jehovah is good; his lovingkindness endureth for ever,

And his faithfulness unto all generations.
